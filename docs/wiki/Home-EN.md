# E-ink Studio — Wiki (English)

*🇳🇱 [Nederlands](Home-NL) · 🌐 [Language picker](Home)*

A **visual editor for ESPHome e-paper displays**, running as a Home Assistant add-on.
Drag elements onto a paper-accurate canvas, bind them to live Home Assistant values, and
generate ready-to-paste ESPHome `display:` lambda + YAML. It is **preview-only** — it
never writes to your device config or your HA states.
